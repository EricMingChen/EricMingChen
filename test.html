Ming Chen

Ming Chen is a PhD student at the School of Education, University of Bristol, UK. He obtained his MA in Digital Technologies, Communication, and Education from The University of Manchester, UK. His research areas focus on learning sciences, technology-enhanced learning, and augmented reality/virtual reality supported language learning. His PhD study is fully funded by China Scholarship Council and University of Bristol Joint Scholarship.

Ming Chen is also interested in teacher education. He actively participated in several research projects in mainland China, notably contributing to researcg projects such as "Exploring the Development of Research Literacy Among Early Career Teachers" conducted at South China Normal University and, "Enhancing Digital Literacy for Rural Teachers in the Digital Age" conducted at Beijing Normal University.
